WHO I AM?

AGE: 20 YEARS OLD

Hi, I’m Sarthik Maity, a 20-year-old from Kolkata with a foot in two different worlds: Technology and Design. Currently, I’m pursuing my Bachelor’s in CSE (AI & Machine Learning). While I spend a lot of my time understanding algorithms and data, I’ve always believed that even the best tech needs to look good. That’s where my creative side comes in. When I’m not coding, you’ll usually find me in Photoshop or editing videos. I’m a huge football fan, and most of my design work is centered around that—creating high-energy posters of my favorite players.
